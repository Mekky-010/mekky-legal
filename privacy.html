VORTYX Privacy Policy

Effective Date: June 14, 2026

Last Updated: June 14, 2026

1. Introduction

VORTYX is a fitness coaching and workout tracking application. This Privacy Policy explains what information VORTYX stores, how it is used, and what rights you have over your data.

VORTYX stores your fitness data, first name, and training history locally on your device. It does not transmit your personal or fitness data to VORTYX servers, does not sell your data, and does not share it with third parties.

2. Information We Collect

VORTYX stores information you enter directly, including your first name, training goal, workout logs, sets, repetitions, weight selections, optional effort ratings, and daily readiness selections.

VORTYX may also generate information from your usage, including personal records, training profile data, coaching decisions, and local app state. This information is stored locally on your device.

VORTYX does not collect location data, camera or microphone input, payment information, Apple Health or Google Fit data, advertising identifiers, analytics data, or sensitive medical records.

3. How Information Is Used

Your data is used only to provide VORTYX features, including workout tracking, coaching recommendations, progression guidance, recovery interpretation, training profile generation, and personal record tracking.

VORTYX does not use your data for advertising, external profiling, analytics, or selling user information.

4. Local Storage and Device Data

All VORTYX data is stored in your browser or app local storage on your device. VORTYX does not operate a server that stores your workout data.

Your data remains on your device unless you choose to export it yourself using the built-in export feature.

5. Export and Backup Data

VORTYX includes an optional export and import feature. When you export your data, a JSON backup file is downloaded directly to your device. No data is uploaded to VORTYX servers.

When you import a backup, the selected file is read locally on your device and restored into local storage.

6. Data Retention

VORTYX stores data for as long as it remains in your browser or app storage. You can remove your data by clearing browser or app storage, or by uninstalling the app.

Because VORTYX does not store your data on a server, there is no server-side deletion process.

7. Third-Party Services

VORTYX may load external resources from third-party services during normal use. These services may receive your IP address as part of standard browser requests, but VORTYX does not send them your workout data, name, training history, or coaching data.

Service Purpose Data Exposure
Google Fonts Font loading IP address only through standard HTTP requests.
Cloudinary Exercise demonstration videos IP address only when exercise videos are loaded.
jsDelivr Chart.js library delivery IP address only through standard HTTP requests.

8. Security

VORTYX uses a local-first architecture. Since your fitness data is not stored on VORTYX servers, there is no VORTYX server database containing your workout history.

You are responsible for protecting your device and any exported backup files.

9. Children's Privacy

VORTYX is not directed at children under the age of 13, or under 16 in the European Economic Area. VORTYX does not knowingly collect information from children.

Because all data is stored locally on the user's device and VORTYX has no server-side access to that data, VORTYX cannot directly access or remove any locally stored information. If you believe a child has used VORTYX on a shared device, all stored data can be removed by clearing the browser or app storage on that device, or by uninstalling the app.

For questions about our privacy practices, contact us using the information in Section 12.

10. Your Rights

Depending on your location, you may have rights regarding your personal data. Because VORTYX stores data locally on your device and does not transmit it to a server, you control your data directly.

You can access your data using the export feature, delete your data by clearing app or browser storage, and move your data using the exported JSON backup file.

Because VORTYX does not operate a server that holds your data, we cannot fulfill access or deletion requests for server-held data because none exists.

11. Changes to This Policy

If this Privacy Policy changes, the Last Updated date will be revised. Material changes to how VORTYX handles data will be communicated before those changes take effect.
